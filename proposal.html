Mr. Eisenhuth's Resume Website

Kurt Eisenhuth

Purpose and Goals

The purpose of my website is to showcase my resume in an online environment. This will be posted to my Github in the future. I would like to move on from System Administration to Cloud Engineering.

Target Audience

My target audience will be all future employers. I would like peers, managers, and friends to view my resume site. The more people that view and share my resume, the more exposure I will get. Any peer or friend that will be visiting my website will be able to share and possibly recommend job opportunities. Any hiring managers that may view my resume site can include it in a possible application. This website will be an addition to my actual resume. The more skills I have available, the better chances I have at moving forward.

Competitor Analysis with Examples

There are many others that have a website to show off their skills. Standing out in a crowded field can be done by adding unique things to my portfolio and website. I can show past projects that have been successfully completed. I can also include some failures and how I responded to them.

Content Strategy

I want to make some smooth transitions into each section. I would like to use my own images. I might use some content from the internet, but I’m not decided on how many images will be included in this website.

Navigation

This will be a 1 page website with scrolling transitions. The headers for each section will be linked. In the future I will be adding other projects inside of this website, but for this class, it will be just this single page. Maybe I could add some sample projects for the other languages I’m learning just as place holders or examples.

Font Families

I used the fonts Raleway and Assistant. I am going for a professional look. I am also not a fan of the serif fonts. I prefer the sans serif fonts for the cleaner look.
